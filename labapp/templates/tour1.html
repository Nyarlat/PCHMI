New Year's hike in the Urals

Chelyabinsk region and the Urals

Winter in the Urals is real, snowy, beautiful. A hike in the Taganay National Park is a great opportunity to see all this, enjoy the romance of snow-covered mountains and forests with comfort, since all overnight stays will be in heated houses - no one will freeze!

Tour program

  • 1 day
    Meeting. Departure to Taganay. Preparing for a hike

    We meet in Chelyabinsk at 12:50 at the Museum of the Southern Urals, first of all we will study the history of the places we plan to visit.

    After the tour, we get into the transfer and go to Taganay.

    There is no need to go anywhere on foot today, we arrive at the Central Estate and stay here for the night.

    This is the main gate of the park, from which all official routes originate.

    Let's go through registration and get the necessary permits, then check into the houses, get to know each other, have the first joint dinner and sleep.

  • 2 day
    Taganay spring "White Key". Climbing the mountain Two-headed Sopka

    The first transition under a backpack and a meeting of the New Year (for a group on New Year's dates).

    On the way, we will visit the White Key shelter, where the most famous Taganay spring “White Key” is located with the purest, clear and very tasty water.

    An iron staircase (200 steps) is conveniently located next to the source, next to which we will leave our backpacks and lightly start climbing Mount Dvukhlavaya Sopka.

    Rocks peep through the tops of trees like feathers of birds escaping from the mountain. That is why the southern peak of the hill is called "Feathers" (1034 m).

    After admiring the views, we go down to the backpacks, which we already miss, and continue on our way.

    Our goal is the Taganay tourist shelter, which is located in a picturesque place at the foot of Mount Kruglitsa.

    We will go there tomorrow, and today we are resting and preparing for the New Year!

    Crossing 14 km. Height 769 m.
