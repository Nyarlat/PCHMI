About the country

Russia is a country located in Northern Asia and Eastern Europe. It ranks 1st in the world in terms of area, and 9th in terms of population. It borders on 16 states - this is more than any other country.

Climate and seasonality

The country covers 4 climatic zones. Most of it is located in the temperate zone, the northern continental zones and the islands of the Arctic Ocean are in the arctic and subarctic, the Black Sea coast is in the subtropical. The location of the territory in several climatic zones, as well as the features of the relief and proximity/remoteness from the ocean, determine the diversity of the climate: traveling around Russia, you can enjoy hot summers and snowy winters.

Population, language, religion

More than 160 peoples live on the territory of the Russian Federation. Russians, Tatars, Ukrainians, Bashkirs and Chuvashs are represented in large numbers. The official language is Russian, but in some regions national languages have also been preserved. Russia is multi-confessional, Orthodoxy prevails among religions.
