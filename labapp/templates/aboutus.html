ABOUT US

HEART OF RUSSIA

Heart of Russia is a company that appeared in 2022 and was engaged in organizing tourist trips to several regions of Russia. To date, we have covered the whole of Russia and every year thousands of tourists from different countries discover Russia with us. We are included in the register of verified tour operators of Rostourism and are constantly improving the product of our work. Quality, stability and constant progress are our key to success.
Our company offers trips to all regions of Russia, from the harsh beauties of the Russian North to the sunny Caucasus, from the shores of the Crimea to the volcanoes of Kamchatka. During your travels, you will discover unexplored places, the nature of the vast expanses of Russia and the hospitality of local residents. The site offers various types of recreation from quiet excursions to various types of outdoor activities such as hiking, rafting, horseback riding and even dog sledding!
