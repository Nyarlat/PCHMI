Hiking in the Taganay Winter Park
Chelyabinsk region and the Urals
Taganay ridge can be called one of the most picturesque corners of the Urals. A real kingdom of rocks and stone rivers, mountain streams, impassable taiga and mountain tundra. A mountain country, unique in its beauty, covered with many legends and legends. Taganay attracts some with the beauty of pristine nature, others are looking for mysterious oracles of the legendary Hyperborea here. The mythical Chud people keep priceless treasures of rare minerals and precious stones in the depths of the Taganay mountains. Taganay is a mountain country unique in its beauty with the greatest natural resources, an interesting history and still unsolved mysteries.
Tour program
1st day
Two-headed Hill and Rattlesnake key crossing

Meeting in Chelyabinsk until 09:00. Transfer to Zlatoust, NP Taganay.

At the entrance to the park, we register and begin the active part of the route.

After a few kilometers, the bolshaya Tesma River will meet on our way.

From the river we are expected to climb to the White Key spring, there is also a tourist shelter with the same name.

A small snack.

We will leave our backpacks at the shelter and make a radial ascent to one of the peaks of the Two-headed Hill (feathers).

After the descent, we have to go (3 km.) to the Rattlesnake Creek, where we will spend the night.

2nd day
Transition to the Response ridge and Kialimsky cordon

We continue our way to the rock of the Response Ridge (1155 m). This is a rock ridge with a length of more than 1 km, and a height of more than 100 meters.

We have to climb it.

From the bolshaya Tesma River to the Response Ridge, we went up all the time, now we will go down to the old Kialim road to the Taganay shelter.

And then along the old Kialim road we will reach the "Kialim cordon".

We'll stay in it for the night.

3rd day
Ascent of Dalny Taganay and Itsyl

After breakfast, we will make a radial ascent to the Dalny Taganay mountain.

There is a high-altitude weather station "Taganai Gora" on the top, at this time it is no longer working, and is a shelter for tourists.

As the altitude increases, the vegetation changes, starting with the taiga and ending with the high-altitude tundra.

Upon returning to the cordon, we will have a snack and go to the radial exit to the city of Itsyl.

4th day
Passage shelter "Taganay", climbing Kruglitsa

Today we will go back along the Kialim road to the Taganay shelter.

We will leave our backpacks and climb Kruglitsa Mountain, the highest point of Taganay Park.

From the top there are views of all the peaks of the park.

5th day
Transfer to the Central Estate

We return to the beginning of the route.

We will pass through the stone rivers, cross the bolshaya Tesma River and go out to the central estate.

This will be the end of our route.

Overnight stay.

Bathhouse.

6th day
Return to Chelyabinsk

11:00 – 14:00 Transfer from the Central Estate to Chelyabinsk.

We remember and fix in memory for a long time all the most interesting moments of your trip.

In order to keep the beauty of the Ural Mountains in your heart all year round.

The length of the active part of the route is 65 km.

The route or the schedule of the route can be adjusted on the spot, based on weather conditions, the readiness of the group and other circumstances.
