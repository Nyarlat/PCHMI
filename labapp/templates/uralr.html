Weekly rafting on the Chusovaya river
Ural, Perm region
Weekly rafting on the legendary river Chusovaya. The Chusovaya River is the most famous river in the Urals with amazingly beautiful nature and rich history. Hundreds of picturesque rocks rise along the banks of Chusovaya, by the way, the rocks on Chusovaya have long been called stones. On this tour you will be enchanted to admire nature, enjoy the rest from the bustle of the city and make new acquaintances.
Tour program

Let's pass by the Yellow and Red stones, Kharenka's stone, a rift in front of the Rain's stone - there is a small cave in it, where the remains of a shaman were found.
